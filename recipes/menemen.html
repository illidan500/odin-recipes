Menemen

Description

Turkish traditional dish for every meal, find the excellent taste without too much effort.

Ingredients

  • At least two eggs for each portion
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put some virgin olive oil to the pan and heat it strong.
  2. Chop your vegetables into cubes, and cook them until the onions start to change their color into the pink.
  3. Now break your eggs and mix it with your cooked vegetables shortly and it's ready.
